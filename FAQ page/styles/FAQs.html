Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What is OntarioEvents.com?

This website will tell you about different events happening inside the Ontario. You can book the tickets online here in advance.

How OntarioEvents is different from other event portals?

This portal will you each and every diffrent type of events going to happen in Ontario. You can have knowledge about the events and can book tickets online here if you find any event interesting.

What kind of tickets are used?

After booking, an copy of ticket is sent to your email address, you will have to print out that copy and have to take along with you while attending the event.

Booking and Cancellation

How can I book ticket through OntarioEvents?

You will need to log in your account. If you are new, first create one. Then, choose the events you want to see and book them by making payments.

How can I cancel my booking?

First, log In into this website, then choose the 'cancel event' option and your booking will be cancelled.

What is your cancellation policy?

If you change your mind on certain event, you can cancel it. If you cancel within 24 hours of the booking, all the amount paid will be refunded. However, if you cancel it after that then 20% of the amount paid is deducted and the rest is refunded.

What is your refund policy?

All the refunds are processed within 10 days of cancellation.

Payments

What form of payment is accepted?

We accept VISA or MASTER CARD payments.

Do you offer any kind of discount?

Yes, we give 20% discount on every events to the students.

COVID-19 guidelines

What covid-19 protocols do you follow?

While attending the event you need to wear masks all the time and maintain the social distance. Also, you have to show the covid vaccination proof at the entry.
